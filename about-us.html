About Vivo Real Estate Group

Vivo Real Estate Group, Inc. stands as a premier boutique commercial real estate firm in South Florida, renowned for its unparalleled expertise in industrial brokerage, development, and asset management.

2002

Founded in 2002 by Rene Vivo, a seasoned industry leader with over

30 years of experience,

the firm was established to meet the region's demand for a client-centric approach to real estate solutions.

From its inception, Vivo Real Estate Group has set itself apart by blending deep local market knowledge with personalized service.

Under Rene Vivo's visionary leadership, the firm has developed over 2.5 million square feet of industrial space, consistently delivering projects of exceptional value and quality.

Recognized as one of South Florida’s top commercial real estate firms, Vivo Real Estate Group is celebrated for its ability to determine the highest and best use for vacant land and to navigate the complexities of entitling properties for development.

The firm also boasts

Powerful Capital Markets division

This division provides tailored financial solutions and strategic advisory services that empower clients to maximize the value of their investments. By leveraging market insights and financial acumen, the Capital Markets team ensures seamless execution of complex transactions, cementing Vivo Real Estate Group's position as a leader in the commercial real estate finance space.

The firm’s accolades include multiple awards from the CoStar Group and the South Florida Business Journal, reflecting its commitment to excellence and its role as a trusted advisor for family-owned portfolios, institutional investors, and multinational corporations alike.

Vivo Real Estate Group’s comprehensive services encompass acquisitions, dispositions, leasing, tenant representation, and development, making it a one-stop solution for all commercial real estate needs.

Vivo Real Estate Group continues to shape South Florida’s industrial landscape.

Beyond its business achievements, the firm fosters a culture of integrity, innovation, and client satisfaction, ensuring long-term success for its partners and stakeholders.

As South Florida’s leading industrial boutique firm, Vivo Real Estate Group remains dedicated to delivering customized real estate strategies that drive growth and add value, cementing its reputation as a cornerstone of the region’s commercial real estate market.
